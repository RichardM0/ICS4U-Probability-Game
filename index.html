1. One deck has the first 6 even numbers, the other first 6 odd.

2. Click generate to start rolling. Click stop to randomly choose two cards.

3. Add the two numbers. Player one earns one point if sum of the digits is divisible by 4.

4. For player two, if one card is a factor of the other, Player Two is awarded a point.

5. Players will only receive points on their respective turns

6. Player one's score is on the left, Player two on the right.
